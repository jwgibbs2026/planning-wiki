# Cotswolds District Council

Cotswolds District Council is the Local Planning Authority for the Cotswolds district — a large, predominantly rural area covering much of the Cotswolds, including the Cotswolds National Landscape (formerly AONB).

## Ecological Significance

The district contains:
- The **Cotswolds National Landscape** — the largest AONB in England and Wales
- Extensive calcareous grasslands — priority habitat for wildflowers, butterflies, and other invertebrates
- Ancient woodlands across the scarp and valleys
- The River Windrush and its tributaries — important for otters and water voles
- [[SSSI|SSSIs]] including Cleeve Common (the largest remaining area of unimproved limestone grassland in the Cotswolds)
