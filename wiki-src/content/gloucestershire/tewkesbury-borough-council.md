# Tewkesbury Borough Council

Tewkesbury Borough Council is the Local Planning Authority for the Tewkesbury Borough — a large rural area covering the northern part of Gloucestershire including the town of Tewkesbury and the Severn/Avon flood plain.

## Ecological Significance

The borough contains:
- Extensive [[SSSI|SSSIs]] on the Severn and Avon flood plains — internationally important for wintering wildfowl and wading birds
- Traditional flood meadows — among the rarest habitats in England
- Tewkesbury itself is at the confluence of the Severn and Avon — significant flood plain habitat

## Planning Portal

Planning applications for Tewkesbury Borough can be searched on the Tewkesbury Borough Council planning portal.

## Local Plan

Tewkesbury is preparing a joint **Strategic and Local Plan** with Cheltenham Borough Council and Gloucester City Council.
