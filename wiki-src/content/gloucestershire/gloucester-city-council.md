# Gloucester City Council

Gloucester City Council is the Local Planning Authority for Gloucester city. It is a small but densely populated authority covering the historic city of Gloucester.

## Planning Portal

Planning applications for Gloucester City can be searched on the Gloucester City Council planning portal.

## Local Plan

Gloucester City is preparing a joint **Strategic and Local Plan** together with Cheltenham Borough Council and Tewkesbury Borough Council.

Following the community engagement campaign led by Gloucestershire Wildlife Trust in early 2024, Gloucester City Council was one of the last district councils in Gloucestershire to declare a nature emergency (following Cheltenham in March 2024).

## Neighbourhood Plans

There are currently no made or in-development Neighbourhood Development Plans within Gloucester City's boundaries. Community influence on planning is best directed at the Local Plan level.
