# GWT Planning Wiki

A practical reference for anyone involved in planning decisions that affect nature and wildlife in Gloucestershire — whether you're a resident, a parish councillor, a developer, or an ecologist.

Use the sections below to explore, or search for a specific topic using the search bar.
