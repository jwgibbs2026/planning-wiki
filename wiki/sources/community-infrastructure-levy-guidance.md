# Community Infrastructure Levy — Guidance

**Source type**: National planning guidance (MHCLG)
**URL**: https://www.gov.uk/guidance/community-infrastructure-levy
**Legislation**: Planning Act 2008; CIL Regulations 2010 (as amended)

## Summary

Comprehensive guidance on how the Community Infrastructure Levy works, including charging schedules, reliefs and exemptions, collection, and the neighbourhood portion.

## Key Points

- Applies to most new development creating ≥100 sq m net additional floor space, or a new dwelling
- Rates set by LPA charging schedule (£/sq m); must balance development support with viability
- Neighbourhood portion: 25% of CIL receipts passed to parish/town council where NDP is in place (15% without)
- Commencement notice required before work begins for most exemptions
- Mandatory reliefs: social housing, charitable development, minor development, residential extensions
- Discretionary relief: self-build, exceptional circumstances

## Exemptions Requiring Pre-Commencement Notice

- Charitable development
- Social housing
- Self-build
- Exceptional circumstances

Failure to submit commencement notice → 20% surcharge (capped at £2,500).
