# Neighbourhood Plans — Gloucestershire Status

This page records the current status of Neighbourhood Development Plans across Gloucestershire's six local authority areas. Data sourced from the GWT Neighbourhood Nature Planning Toolkit (2024/25).

> **Note:** This data should be verified against each council's planning portal as plans are regularly updated — made, reviewed, or newly designated.

---

## How to Use This Information

If your parish has a **made plan**, review it to see whether it contains strong nature policies. If not, it may be time to review it.

If your parish has a plan **in development**, now is the time to engage — policies are still being written.

If your parish has **no plan**, consider whether starting one is viable. Contact Gloucestershire Wildlife Trust for support.
