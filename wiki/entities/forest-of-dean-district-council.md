# Forest of Dean District Council

Forest of Dean District Council is the Local Planning Authority for the Forest of Dean district — a largely rural area with a rich industrial heritage and internationally recognised woodland habitat.

## Ecological Significance

The Forest of Dean contains:
- The **Forest of Dean SSSI** and associated [[SSSI|SSSIs]] — internationally important for breeding birds (especially raptors) and ancient deadwood invertebrates
- **Wye Valley AONB** (now Wye Valley National Landscape) along the southern border
- Key Protected Species populations including all UK bat species, breeding peregrines, and notable invertebrates

## Planning Portal

Planning applications for the Forest of Dean can be searched on the Forest of Dean District Council planning portal.

## Local Plan

Forest of Dean has an adopted Local Plan and is in the process of updating it.
