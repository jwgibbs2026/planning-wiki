# Secretary of State

The Secretary of State for Housing, Communities and Local Government (MHCLG) is the senior government minister responsible for the planning system in England. The role sets overall planning policy through the [[NPPF]] and has ultimate authority over the most significant planning decisions.

## Key Responsibilities in Planning

- Publishing and updating the [[NPPF]]
- Deciding **called-in** planning applications — where a major or controversial application is "called in" from the Local Planning Authority for national-level determination
- Receiving recommendations from the Planning Inspectorate on Nationally Significant Infrastructure Projects
- Overseeing the general functioning of the planning system

## Called-In Applications

Although rare, the Secretary of State can call in a planning application and decide it personally (with a recommendation from the Planning Inspectorate). This tends to happen for applications with significant national implications.
