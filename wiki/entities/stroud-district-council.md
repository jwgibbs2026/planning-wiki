# Stroud District Council

Stroud District Council is the Local Planning Authority for the Stroud district — a large rural and semi-rural area covering Stroud town, the Cotswold escarpment, the Severn Vale, and the Five Valleys.

## Planning Portal

Planning applications for Stroud District can be searched on the Stroud District Council planning portal.

## Local Plan

Stroud District has an adopted Local Plan and is working on updates. The district includes significant ecological assets including:
- Cotswold AONB (now the Cotswolds National Landscape)
- Ancient woodlands in the Stroud valleys
- Lowland meadows in the Severn Vale
- The River Severn corridor

## Neighbourhood Plans

Stroud District has one of the highest numbers of made Neighbourhood Development Plans in Gloucestershire. See Neighbourhood Plans — Gloucestershire Status for current status.
