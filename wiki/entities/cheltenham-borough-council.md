# Cheltenham Borough Council

Cheltenham Borough Council is the Local Planning Authority for Cheltenham. It is a borough council covering the town of Cheltenham and surrounding areas.

## Planning Portal

Planning applications for Cheltenham can be searched on the Cheltenham Borough Council planning portal.

## Local Plan

Cheltenham is preparing a joint **Strategic and Local Plan** together with Gloucester City Council and Tewkesbury Borough Council. This replaces the previous Joint Core Strategy (JCS).

In March 2024, Cheltenham Borough Council **declared a nature emergency** — the first of the Gloucestershire districts to do so, following a mobilisation campaign by Gloucestershire Wildlife Trust and over 500 community responses to the strategic plan consultation.
